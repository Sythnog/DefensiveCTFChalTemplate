Defensive CTF Judge

Aka. the attacker/validator for "{{ .challName }}"

How the judge works:

  • You press the "START" button, and wait for some time while the judge is working.
  • The judge will attack your machine, to check if you solved the vulnerabilities
  • Furthermore, the judge will test if the normal functionality works as expected.
  • If You solved the vulnerability, then you will be granted the flag.
